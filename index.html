Top 10 Best Characters in Smash Bros Ultimate

  1. Joker

    Joker is known for his speed and versatility. His Rebel's Guard and Arsene make him a formidable opponent.

    • Gun: A ranged attack that deals 3% damage.
    • Eiha: A curse attack that deals 5% damage initially and additional damage over time.
    • Rebel's Guard: A counter move that charges his Arsene meter.
    • Arsene: Enhances all of Joker's attacks.

    Combos: Down Throw to Up Air, Forward Air to Back Air, and Down Throw to Up Smash.

  2. Peach

    Peach is a versatile character with strong aerial attacks and excellent recovery. Her float ability allows for unique combos.

    • Toad: A counter move that deals 12% damage.
    • Peach Bomber: A charging attack that deals 15% damage.
    • Vegetable: Throws a vegetable that deals 8% damage.
    • Parasol: A recovery move that deals 10% damage.

    Combos: Down Throw to Forward Air, Up Tilt to Up Air, and Down Throw to Back Air.

  3. Pikachu

    Pikachu is a fan-favorite with quick moves and powerful electric attacks. His small size and agility make him hard to hit.

    • Thunder Jolt: A projectile attack that deals 6% damage.
    • Skull Bash: A charging attack that deals 10-30% damage depending on charge.
    • Quick Attack: A fast movement attack that deals 3% damage per hit.
    • Thunder: A powerful electric attack that deals 12-30% damage.

    Combos: Down Throw to Up Air, Quick Attack to Thunder, and Forward Air to Skull Bash.

  4. Fox

    Fox McCloud is a fast and agile fighter with strong aerial attacks. His speed and precision make him a top choice for aggressive playstyles.

    • Blaster: A rapid-fire projectile that deals 2% damage per shot.
    • Fox Illusion: A quick dash attack that deals 8% damage.
    • Fire Fox: An explosive charge attack that deals 16% damage.
    • Reflector: Reflects projectiles and deals 5% damage.

    Combos: Down Throw to Up Tilt, Up Air to Back Air, and Down Throw to Up Smash.

  5. Lucina

    Lucina is a balanced character with strong sword attacks and excellent spacing tools. Her moveset is straightforward and effective.

    • Shield Breaker: A charging attack that deals 8-27% damage.
    • Dancing Blade: A multi-hit attack that deals 3-15% damage.
    • Counter: A counter move that deals 1.2x the opponent's attack damage.
    • Up Special: A recovery move that deals 13% damage.

    Combos: Down Throw to Forward Air, Up Tilt to Up Air, and Down Throw to Back Air.

  6. Steve

    Steve from Minecraft is a unique character with the ability to build blocks and craft weapons. His playstyle is creative and strategic.

    • Mine: Gathers resources to craft stronger weapons.
    • Craft: Crafts weapons and tools to enhance attacks.
    • Block: Builds blocks to control the battlefield.
    • Minecart: A projectile attack that deals 12% damage.

    Combos: Down Throw to Up Air, Forward Air to Back Air, and Down Throw to Up Smash.

  7. Pyra

    Pyra is a powerful swordfighter with strong close-range attacks. Her aggressive playstyle can overwhelm opponents.

    • Flame Nova: A spinning attack that deals 15% damage.
    • Blazing End: A projectile attack that deals 12% damage.
    • Prominence Revolt: A recovery move that deals 10% damage.
    • Burning Sword: A powerful sword attack that deals 20% damage.

    Combos: Down Throw to Forward Air, Up Tilt to Up Air, and Down Throw to Back Air.

  8. Mario

    Mario is the iconic mascot of Nintendo and a versatile fighter with balanced abilities. His moveset is easy to learn but hard to master, making him a favorite for both beginners and seasoned players.

    • Fireball: A projectile attack that deals 5% damage.
    • Super Jump Punch: An upward punch that deals 15% damage.
    • Cape: A counter move that reflects projectiles and deals 8% damage.
    • F.L.U.D.D.: A water-based attack that pushes opponents but deals no damage.

    Combos: Down Throw to Up Tilt, Up Air to Forward Air, and Down Throw to Forward Air.

  9. Ness

    Ness is a psychic-powered kid from EarthBound with a variety of moves, from long-range attacks using PSI to short-range attacks with his bat and yo-yo. His aerial game and grab game are among the best in the cast.

    • PK Fire: A projectile attack that deals 10% damage and traps opponents.
    • PK Thunder: A guided attack that deals 8-25% damage depending on charge.
    • PSI Magnet: Absorbs projectiles to heal Ness and deals 10% damage.
    • PK Flash: A powerful attack that deals 10-37% damage depending on charge.

    Combos: Down Throw to Up Air, PK Fire to Forward Air, and Down Throw to Back Air

  10. R.O.B.

    R.O.B. is a versatile character with powerful projectiles and excellent recovery. His moveset allows him to play both offensively and defensively.

    • Robo Beam: A laser attack that deals 5-22% damage depending on charge.
    • Gyro: A spinning top that deals 6-10.8% damage depending on charge.
    • Arm Rotor: A spinning attack that deals 10-20% damage.
    • Robo Burner: A recovery move that deals no damage but provides excellent mobility.
    • Super Arm: A powerful grab attack that deals 12-20% damage.

    Combos: Down Throw to Up Air, Gyro to Forward Air, and Down Throw to Up Smash.
